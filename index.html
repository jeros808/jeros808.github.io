+ABOUT

Hello World, my name is Jeff Rosal, I am a UX/UI Designer, UX Researcher, Frontend Developer and Artist from Chicago, Illinois. As an Artist I have worked in Creative Direction, Design, Studio Photography, Video and Sound. At The School of The Art Institute of Chicago, I studied Human Computer Interaction, I then went to the StartUp Institute, where I studied UX/UI Design and Frontend development. I love making things with code, microcontrollers, and electronics. I am really interested in cultures, philosophy, psychology, and science. The last books I read were "The Tao of Pooh" AND "How to Create a Mind".

Some skills I have acquired through interest - HTML, CSS, Javascript, JAVA, C, Python, Visual Basic, mySQL, Processing.js, 3D Modeling, Google Analytics, SEO, Web Scrapping, Wit.AI, Twitter bots, Unity Video Game Engine, Bootstrap, Foundation, Github, Wordpress, Adobe Creative Suite, Arduino, Lean UX and Skateboarding. Currently, I work Fulltime at Cultivate Brand Agency and attend Northwestern University for Data Science - WANT TO LEARN MORE? PLEASE CONTACT ME HERE.
