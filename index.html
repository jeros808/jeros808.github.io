+ABOUT

Hello World, my name is Jeff, I am a UX/UI Designer and Researcher from Chicago. As an Artist I have worked in Creative Direction, Design, Studio Photography, Video and Sound. At The School of The Art Institute of Chicago, I studied Human Computer Interaction, I then went to the StartUp Institute, where I studied UX/UI Design and Frontend development. I love making things with code, microcontrollers, and electronics. I am really interested in cultures, philosophy, psychology, and science. The last books I read were "The Tao of Pooh" AND "How to Create a Mind".

Some skills I have acquired through interest - HTML, CSS, Javascript, JAVA, C, Processing.js, 3D Modeling, Unity Video Game Engine, Bootstrap, Foundation, Github, Wordpress, Adobe Creative Suite, Arduino. WANT TO LEARN MORE? PLEASE CONTACT ME HERE.
