+ABOUT

Hello World, my name is Jeff Rosal, I am a UX/UI Designer, Researcher, Fullstack Developer and Technology Artist from Chicago, Illinois. As an Artist I have worked in Creative Direction, Design, Studio Photography, Video and Sound. At The School of The Art Institute of Chicago, I studied Human Computer Interaction and Computer Science. Recently, I attended Northwestern University for Data Science. I love making things with code, microcontrollers, and electronics. I am really interested in cultures, philosophy, psychology, and science. The last books I read were "The Tao of Pooh" AND "How to Create a Mind".

Some skills I have acquired through interest - HTML, CSS, Javascript, JAVA, C, Python, Visual Basic, mySQL, Processing.js, 3D Modeling, Google Analytics, SEO, Web Scrapping, Wit.AI, Twitter bots, Unity Video Game Engine, Bootstrap, Foundation, Github, Wordpress, Adobe Creative Suite, Arduino, Lean UX and Skateboarding. Currently, I work at Northwestern as an Assistant Instructor and Apparel Agency as a Lead Engineer. - WANT TO LEARN MORE?
