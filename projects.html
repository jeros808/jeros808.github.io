Zen Hero - 2010

What is Control, How much can we control nature, Just let go. This game has no levels, it changes speed based on how calm you are and the piece rotates based on how well you can control your emotions


  • The interface enclosure houses
  • 2- arduinos, one of which is used as a pulserate monitor and one which is a biofeedback monitor.
  • 1- hacked USB gamepad,
  • 1- 10 watt amplifier,
  • 1- 6 inch speaker,
  • 1- laptop


Lineages - 2008

A Java app for an installation that summarizes web articles about Lineages. I am a River words flows through me. Words are like viruses, trending, hashtagged...... We filter and change the meanings of words to fit our personal ideals, are likeness. These works are about Etymology, Identity, and Artificial Intelligence...Constructivist Learning vs Machine learning...



Project_litterbox.exe - 2012

Litterbox is a quirky, non malicious Mouse/Cursor take over, "art virus", A little cute kitten chasing an uncontrollable mouse. Can it be stopped? Do you wanna stop it? Its hard to stop a mouse when it is being chased.



DataMine Your Life - 2014

Data mining is the use of collected data for analysis. It can sometimes be seen as spyware but its uses can be seen in Facebook ads. non the less it makes people uncomfortable. We all make sounds. What if our Data was collected through Sound. This work is about Prosody and What frequencies make us feel happy or sad? How does the Sound of a room full people effect our emotions? And do those emotions spread? DataMine Your Life is an Interactive installation that deals with the discovery patterns in a hive mind like environment, Like a game of telephone. Using a Condenser Microphone, a collection of carrier sound frequency data is taken from the room but not before the sounds travels through us, the data is collected and cued to reveal advertisements queried from Google.
